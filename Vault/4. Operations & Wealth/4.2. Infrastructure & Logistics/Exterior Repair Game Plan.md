# 2005 Honda Pilot: Exterior Repair Game Plan

This guide outlines the repair strategy for the driver-side rear quarter panel and bumper damage.

---

## 1. Vehicle Specifications
* **Model:** 2005 Honda Pilot
* **Likely Color:** Satin Silver Metallic (**NH623M**) or Alabaster Silver Metallic (**NH700M**).
* **Action:** Check the sticker on your driver’s side door jamb to confirm the 6-character paint code before ordering supplies.

---

## 2. Option A: The "Budget Savior" (DIY)
**Estimated Cost:** $150 – $300
**Goal:** Structural integrity and "10-foot" aesthetics (looks good from a distance).

### Steps:
1. **Pop the Dent:** Remove the interior trunk trim to access the backside of the panel. Use a rubber mallet and a block of wood to push the metal out with steady pressure.
2. **Pull the Crease:** Use a bridge dent puller kit for the areas you can't reach from behind.
3. **Sand & Smooth:** Sand deep scratches with 180-grit sandpaper. Apply a thin layer of Bondo (Body Filler). Sand smooth with 320-grit.
4. **Bumper Patch:** If the bumper is just cracked, use a plastic bumper repair epoxy on the inside to stabilize it.
5. **Touch-Up Paint:** Use a color-matched aerosol spray can followed by a clear coat.

### Shopping List:
* [ ] Bondo All-Purpose Putty Kit (~$15)
* [ ] Hand Sanding Block & Sandpaper Variety Pack (80, 180, 320, 600 grit) (~$20)
* [ ] Aerosol Paint Kit (NH623M/NH700M) (~$50)
* [ ] Suction Cup / Bridge Dent Puller (~$25)

---

## 3. Option B: The "Clean Refresh" (Semi-DIY)
**Estimated Cost:** $500 – $800
**Goal:** A high-quality finish without professional labor rates.

### Steps:
1. **Replace the Bumper:** Order a **pre-painted** rear bumper cover. This avoids the difficulty of painting large plastic parts and ensures a factory-level shine.
2. **Professional Metal Work:** Hire a mobile Paintless Dent Repair (PDR) specialist to handle the quarter panel. They can often get the metal 90% back to its original shape.
3. **Final Finish:** Use a DIY touch-up kit only for the remaining small creases.

### Shopping List:
* [ ] Pre-Painted Bumper Cover: Search "2005 Honda Pilot Rear Bumper Painted [Your Color Code]" (~$350–$450).
* [ ] Bumper Retainer Clips: 10-pack of Honda-specific clips (~$12).

---

## 4. Step-by-Step Quarter Panel "Inside-Out" Fix
If you choose the DIY route (Option A), follow these specific steps:

1. **Access:** Open the liftgate and pull back the carpet/plastic trim on the driver's side cargo area.
2. **The Push:** Have a friend watch the outside while you push from the inside. Stop if you hear the paint cracking.
3. **Sanding:** Sand the area until the shine is gone to give the filler a "key" to stick to.
4. **Filling:** Apply Bondo in thin layers. It is much easier to add more than to sand off a large lump.
5. **Painting:** Tape off the tail light and fuel door. Spray 3 light coats of Base Color (wait 10 mins between), then 2 coats of Clear Coat (wait 20 mins between).
6. **Curing:** Do not wash the car for at least 48 hours.
